Getting Started

2. Browse Cases

Understanding Rarity

COMMON Most frequent drops. Found in every case.
UNCOMMON Decent chance of appearing in most cases.
RARE Special Pokémon like starters and pseudo-legendaries.
EPIC Legendary Pokémon from various regions.
LEGENDARY Ultra-rare pulls with special animations.
MYTHICAL The rarest category. Shiny variants are incredibly valuable.

Provably Fair System

Every case opening on KatsuCases is determined by a cryptographically secure random number generator. Here's how it works:

  1. When you open a case, our server generates a random seed.
  2. This seed is combined with your session ID and case contents.
  3. The combined values are hashed to produce the final result.
  4. The entire process is transparent and verifiable.

100% Fair & Random

Every pull has an equal chance. The odds displayed for each item are the exact probabilities, not inflated for marketing.
